Overview

Purpose

To make a gaming blog where I can share my gaming discoveries, be it easter eggs in a game, an analysis of a character arc, or fan content.

Audience

With my color scheme and my general taste in games I would expect this site to appeal most to young adults. I have a fair number of games I play that are older games that not many people talk about, so I would expect to attract an audience of adults in their 50s to 60s as well because I can cater to them in the sense that it is rare to find people talking about the other games I play.
